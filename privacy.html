Privacy Policy for Rezo Music

Rezo Music ("the App," "we," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy applies to the Rezo Music iOS app and the related support pages we operate. It explains what information is processed, how it is used, when it may be shared, your choices, and how to contact us.

In the current version of the App, you can start listening without creating an account, and the App does not maintain a developer-operated user account system.

1. Types of Information We Process

1.1 Information you save locally

The App stores the information you choose to keep on your device so it can remember your listening preferences and local library state.

  • nickname
  • avatar image, if you choose one
  • favorite playlists
  • playback history
  • listening time and other local playback statistics
  • app preferences such as language, visualizer mode, and onboarding completion

1.2 Information you provide when contacting us

If you contact us by email, we receive the email address you use and any information you include in the message.

  • your email address
  • the information you include in your message

1.3 Information we do not intentionally collect

The current version does not intentionally collect contacts, precise location, microphone content, payment information, ad identifiers, or login credentials.

1.4 Support site information

The support site is static and does not use forms or tracking scripts.

1.5 Automated technical information

When you use the App, we may automatically process limited technical information, specifically app launch events, to understand app performance and usage.

2. How We Use Information

  • remember local preferences and profile state
  • display content in the language set on your iPhone
  • support browsing, playback, and offline listening
  • keep local history and listening statistics
  • reply to support emails
  • operate, maintain, improve, and protect the App and related support pages
  • comply with applicable law

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal information, and we do not use local app data for advertising or ad profiling. We only share or disclose limited information in the following circumstances:

3.1 At your direction

For example, when you send a support email, use a system feature, or otherwise ask us to use or disclose information on your behalf.

3.2 With necessary service providers

We may rely on hosting, email, or content infrastructure providers to operate the website and provide public catalog and media resources. They only process the information needed to provide those services.

3.3 As required by law

We may disclose information when we believe disclosure is necessary to comply with applicable law, regulation, a court order, or a lawful request from a government authority.

3.4 To protect rights, interests, or safety

We may take reasonable steps to protect the rights, safety, and property of users, the public, us, or third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • nickname, avatar, favorites, playback history, listening statistics, and preferences are stored on your device
  • the app follows the language set on your iPhone
  • depending on your device settings, some local app data may be included in Apple-managed backups
  • we make reasonable efforts to protect data, but no method of storage or transmission is absolutely secure

5. Your Rights

5.1 Access and view

You can view the profile information and preferences available within the App.

5.2 Correct and update

You can modify your nickname, avatar, and other available settings within the App.

5.3 Delete

You can delete local history or delete the App to remove data stored on your device.

5.4 Reset

If you change the language in iPhone Settings, the App will follow that language.

5.5 Questions and complaints

If you have questions, comments, or requests regarding privacy practices, you may contact us using the contact details below.

6. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time based on product changes, legal requirements, or regulatory requirements. Any updated version will be published on the relevant page and will take effect as of the effective date stated there.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions, comments, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or the App's data handling practices, please contact us at:
