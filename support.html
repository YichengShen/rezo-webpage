Technical support

For playback and app issues

If playback, downloads, language, or the app itself behave unexpectedly, send the details below.

  • Playback stops, stutters, or will not start
  • Downloads do not finish or look outdated
  • Language or layout text looks wrong
  • The app crashes, freezes, or returns to the wrong screen

Include your device model, iOS version, app version, and the steps that led to the issue.

FAQ

Common questions

How do I switch language in the iOS app?
Change the language in your iPhone Settings. Rezo Music follows the language set on your device.
What should I include in a technical report?
Include your device model, iOS version, app version, and the steps that reproduced the issue.
Can I send a music request in Chinese?
Yes. Use whichever language is easier for you.
